Neuraum is different. We kick-started our company with the acquisition of several well-established websites from a 70+ year old internet pioneer. These content-rich, high-traffic platforms build the foundation for developing and growing innovative business models in the space of building, home & living. With our flagship Fertighaus.de we run the biggest German house and building portal helping hundreds of thousands of users to find the home of their dreams. In 2016 that let to already more than 1.000 sold houses via our construction partners. But this is just the beginning in future we will offer a considerably broader and deeper range of services for anyone interested in building.

We are a team of experienced professionals, working with fun and passion on the realization of our ambitious vision. We believe in an agile and self-reliant working style, in sustainable growth and a healthy balance of work and personal life. To support our Product Team, we are currently looking for a full-time

Product Manager (m/w)

 

Your Role

Being a product manager for our key site Fertighaus.de means working on features that change a whole industry and impact the lives of many people. Together with our management and development teams and under the leadership and mentorship of our CPO, you will support us in:

  • Shaping the vision, strategy & roadmap of our product
  • Creating a world-class user experience based on extensive user research, data analysis & competitor benchmarks
  • Instituting a culture of continuous improvement through A/B testing and a thorough review of feature releases
  • Understanding the needs of our users, our construction companies and the characteristics of our market
  • Thinking of innovative and smart ways to improve our recommendation engine
  • Unifying the complex requirements of all stakeholders and business goals

 

Your Profile

  • You have a solid experience in product management or are capable of learning the fundamentals in a very short amount of time
  • You communicate in a clear, structured way and are able to write feature requirements that incorporate both the big picture and the small details
  • You combine a great intuition for UX flow, acknowledging the importance of extensive research for creating a great product
  • You are proactive, self-driven and comfortable with a fast-paced, always-on, highly ambiguous environment and have the potential to make yourself irreplaceable
  • You are eager to learn and keep yourself updated about the newest trends in product development & technologies
  • You have a clear strength and interest, be it UX, user research, analytics, software development, or product processes
  • You love teamwork and include experts from inside or outside the company into product ideation & development process
  • You are very analytical and capable of defining and defending a strong opinion based on facts
  • You are fluent in English and have a good understanding of German
