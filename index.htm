HelloWorld…

Have you ever watched children running around in this world — staring wide eyed at the amazing colors and things around their universe, looking up at the sky and watching themselves in mirrored surfaces?

I wonder what it was like to experience my very first color. After all, it must have been an overwhelming key experience of life. Did it make me happy, or did it make me cry? Did we fully understand the true meaning of it, or was it just another happy thing to look at?

As we grow up, we gradually learn to shut out the onslaught of information; we no longer notice the world around us, as our younger siblings do. By the time adulthood is reached, these sensations are even lost to memory. And sadly enough, we don't seem to miss those key moments of life.

What if we wake up tomorrow and there is a new color? Wouldn't that be awesome! Would we even notice or care? Would we appreciate it, or would it be just another piece of information at this point? Something as simple as a new color, an RGB value, a wavelength so small but yet with the potential to change the world forever.

When you are aware of the world around, you will realize how un-adult your behavior is; you'll be able to rediscover just how it feels to be a kid again.

There is so much beauty in this world, enjoy the silence and don't be afraid to smile :)
